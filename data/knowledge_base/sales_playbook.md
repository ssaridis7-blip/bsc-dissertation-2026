# NexusFlow Solutions - Sales Playbook

## Sales Process Overview

### Deal Stages
Our sales pipeline follows six stages with target progression timelines:

1. **Discovery** (Week 1-2): Initial contact, needs assessment, qualification criteria check
2. **Qualification** (Week 2-3): Budget, authority, need, and timeline (BANT) verification
3. **Proposal** (Week 3-4): Custom proposal creation, pricing negotiation, stakeholder mapping
4. **Negotiation** (Week 4-6): Contract terms discussion, discount approval process, legal review
5. **Closed Won**: Contract signed, handoff to onboarding team
6. **Closed Lost**: Documented reason for loss, scheduled follow-up for future opportunity

### Lead Scoring Model
Our lead scoring system uses a 0-100 scale based on:
- **Firmographic fit** (0-30 points): Company size, industry match, geographic location
- **Engagement level** (0-30 points): Website visits, email opens, content downloads, demo requests
- **BANT criteria** (0-40 points): Budget availability (10), decision-maker access (10), clear need identified (10), timeline defined (10)

Scoring thresholds:
- 80-100: Hot lead - immediate follow-up required within 24 hours
- 60-79: Warm lead - schedule follow-up within 1 week
- 40-59: Nurture - add to email nurture campaign
- 0-39: Cold lead - monitor for engagement signals

## Pricing and Discount Guidelines

### Standard Discounts
- **Annual prepayment**: 5% discount (already included in listed annual pricing)
- **Multi-year commitment**: 10% for 2-year, 15% for 3-year contracts
- **Academic/Non-profit**: Up to 40% discount with verified credentials
- **Partner referral**: 5% discount for referred customers

### Discount Approval Matrix
- Up to 10%: Account Executive can approve
- 11-20%: Sales Manager approval required
- 21-30%: VP Sales approval required
- 31%+: CEO approval required (academic/non-profit exceptions apply)

### Competitive Pricing
When competing against specific platforms:
- **Salesforce**: Emphasize total cost of ownership (implementation, training, customization). Our Professional tier delivers 80% of Salesforce functionality at 40% of the cost for SMEs.
- **HubSpot**: Highlight our superior invoicing and financial reporting capabilities. HubSpot requires separate tools for billing workflows.
- **Pipedrive**: Position our analytics and AI capabilities as key differentiators. Pipedrive lacks enterprise reporting features.

## Objection Handling

### "Your pricing is too high"
Response framework: Acknowledge the concern, then reframe around value. Calculate the ROI: average customer sees 35% improvement in sales team efficiency and 42% faster deal closure. For a 10-person sales team at average UK salary, that translates to £150K+ annual productivity gain. Offer a phased implementation starting with core modules to reduce initial investment.

### "We need more industry-specific features"
Response framework: Highlight our configurable workflow engine that adapts to any industry. Provide relevant case studies from similar industries. Discuss our product roadmap and customer feedback portal where they can influence feature development. For critical features, explore Premium tier custom development hours.

### "We're concerned about data migration risks"
Response framework: Explain our proven migration methodology with 99.7% data integrity rate across 50+ migrations. Offer a parallel-run period where both systems operate simultaneously. Highlight our free migration assistance and dedicated migration specialist. Provide references from customers who migrated from their current platform.

### "We need to see more ROI evidence"
Response framework: Share anonymized case studies with measurable outcomes. Offer an extended proof-of-concept (3 months) with clearly defined success metrics. Propose a pilot with a subset of their team to demonstrate value before full deployment.

## Customer Success Metrics

### Key Performance Indicators
After implementation, customers typically achieve:
- 35% reduction in support ticket resolution time
- 42% improvement in order processing efficiency
- 28% increase in sales team productivity
- 15% improvement in customer retention rates
- Average ROI payback period: 6-8 months

### Quarterly Business Review (QBR) Template
Every customer on Professional and Premium tiers receives quarterly reviews covering:
1. Usage metrics and adoption rates
2. ROI analysis against pre-defined success metrics
3. Feature utilization recommendations
4. Product roadmap preview
5. Expansion opportunities assessment

## Win-Back Strategy
For churned customers:
1. Wait 90 days after churn before re-engagement
2. Lead with new features or improvements addressing their stated exit reasons
3. Offer a "welcome back" discount (up to 20%, Sales Manager approval)
4. Propose a no-commitment 30-day trial of the latest version
5. Assign a senior account executive for the win-back attempt
