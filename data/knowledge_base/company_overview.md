# NexusFlow Solutions - Company Overview

## About Us
NexusFlow Solutions is a UK-based B2B SaaS company specializing in business process automation and customer relationship management tools. Founded in 2019, the company serves small to medium enterprises (SMEs) across multiple industries including technology, financial services, healthcare, logistics, retail, and education.

## Our Products

### NexusFlow CRM Platform
Our flagship product is a cloud-based CRM platform designed for SMEs that need enterprise-grade features without enterprise-level complexity. The platform offers:

- **Contact Management**: Centralized database for managing customer relationships, including interaction history, notes, and lead scoring.
- **Deal Pipeline**: Visual deal tracking from discovery through to close, with customizable stages and probability weighting.
- **Invoicing & Billing**: Integrated invoicing with automated payment reminders and financial reporting.
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Real-time business intelligence including revenue forecasting, team performance metrics, and customer health scores.
- **API Integration Hub**: REST API for connecting with third-party tools including ERP systems, accounting software, and marketing platforms.
