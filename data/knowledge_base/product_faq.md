# NexusFlow CRM - Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is NexusFlow CRM?
NexusFlow CRM is a cloud-based customer relationship management platform designed specifically for small to medium enterprises (SMEs). It combines contact management, deal tracking, invoicing, and analytics into a single integrated platform.

### Who is NexusFlow CRM designed for?
Our platform is designed for B2B companies with 10-500 employees who need a professional CRM solution without the complexity and cost of enterprise platforms like Salesforce or Microsoft Dynamics.

### What industries do you serve?
We serve clients across technology, financial services, healthcare, logistics, retail, education, renewable energy, construction, media and advertising, and food and beverage industries. Our platform is industry-agnostic but includes configurable templates for common industry workflows.

## Technical Questions

### What are the system requirements?
NexusFlow CRM is entirely cloud-based and runs in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). No software installation required. For the on-premise option (Premium tier only), minimum requirements are: 16GB RAM, 4-core CPU, 500GB SSD storage, and Ubuntu 22.04 LTS or Windows Server 2022.

### Do you offer API access?
Yes, our REST API is available on Professional and Premium tiers. The API supports full CRUD operations on contacts, deals, invoices, and interactions. API documentation is available at docs.nexusflow.io/api. Rate limits are 1,000 requests per minute for Professional and 5,000 for Premium.

### Can NexusFlow integrate with my existing systems?
Yes, we offer native integrations with popular tools including: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Google Workspace, QuickBooks, Xero, Mailchimp, HubSpot Marketing, Zapier, and SAP (via middleware). Custom integrations are available through our API or as part of Premium tier custom development hours.

### How does the AI Analytics module work?
The AI Analytics module uses machine learning models trained on anonymized, aggregated business patterns to provide predictive insights. It analyzes your CRM data locally and provides: lead scoring predictions, churn risk alerts, revenue forecasts, and sentiment analysis on customer interactions. All AI processing complies with GDPR and no customer data leaves your designated data region.

### What about data migration from other CRMs?
We provide free data migration assistance for all new customers. We support direct imports from Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho CRM, and CSV/Excel files. Typical migration takes 1-3 business days depending on data volume.

## Security and Compliance

### Is NexusFlow GDPR compliant?
Yes, NexusFlow is fully GDPR compliant. We offer data processing agreements (DPAs), support data subject access requests (DSARs), provide data portability in standard formats, and allow complete data deletion upon request. Our data centers are located in the UK and EU.

### What security certifications do you hold?
We hold ISO 27001 certification and are Cyber Essentials Plus certified. We conduct annual penetration testing by independent third parties and publish the summary results to customers upon request.

### Does NexusFlow meet NHS data standards?
Our Premium tier with on-premise deployment can be configured to meet NHS Digital Data Security and Protection Toolkit requirements. This includes UK-only data residency, full audit trails, role-based access control, and encryption at rest and in transit. Contact our healthcare specialist team for detailed compliance documentation.

### How is customer data protected?
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We implement role-based access control, multi-factor authentication, and maintain detailed audit logs. Backups are performed every 6 hours with 30-day retention. We never share customer data with third parties.
